# HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition Analysis
1. Project Title / Headline
HR Analytics Dashboard | Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights Using Power BI

An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition trends, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and job role performance to support strategic HR decision-making.

2. Purpose
   
The purpose of this dashboard is to help HR teams and management identify the major factors contributing to employee attrition and workforce trends. It enables data-driven decisions to improve employee retention, workforce planning, and organizational performance.

3. Tech Stack (Tools Used)
   
Power BI – Dashboard creation and data visualization
Microsoft Excel / CSV – Data storage and preprocessing
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs
Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

4. Data Source
   
The dataset used for this project is based on HR employee attrition data, containing information such as:

Employee demographics
Department and job roles
Salary slabs
Education background
Years at company
Attrition status
Age and gender details

The data is structured to simulate real-world HR analytics scenarios for workforce analysis.

5. Features / Highlights
   
-Business Problem

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges faced by organizations. High employee turnover increases recruitment costs, affects productivity, and impacts overall organizational growth. HR departments often struggle to identify the exact reasons behind attrition and the employee groups most affected.

-Goal of the Dashboard

The main goal of this dashboard is to:

Monitor overall employee attrition trends
Identify high-risk departments and job roles
Analyze attrition based on age, salary, education, and experience
Support HR managers in making proactive retention strategies
Improve workforce planning through data-driven insights

-Key Visuals & Insights

1.KPI Cards

The dashboard provides quick HR performance indicators including:
Total Employees
Total Attrition Count
Attrition Rate
Average Age
Average Salary
Average Years at Company

These KPIs give management an instant overview of workforce health.

2.Attrition by Gender

A gender-wise attrition comparison helps identify workforce retention differences between male and female employees.
Insight:
Male employees show comparatively higher attrition than female employees.

3.Attrition by Education

This visualization highlights attrition across different educational backgrounds such as:
Human Resources
Technical Degree
Marketing
Life Sciences
Medical

Insight:
Employees from Human Resources and Technical Degree backgrounds contribute significantly to overall attrition.

4.Attrition by Age Group

The dashboard analyzes attrition across various age categories.
Insight:
Employees aged 26–35 years show the highest attrition, indicating that mid-career professionals are more likely to leave the organization.

5.Attrition by Salary Slab

This chart helps understand how salary impacts employee turnover.
Insight:
Employees earning up to 5K salary slab have the highest attrition, suggesting compensation may be a major factor influencing resignations.

6.Attrition by Years at Company

This trend analysis shows attrition based on employee tenure.
Insight:
Most attrition occurs during the early years of employment, highlighting potential issues in employee engagement or onboarding experience.

7.Attrition by Job Role

This section identifies which job roles experience the highest employee turnover.
Insight:
Laboratory Technicians
Sales Executives
Research Scientists
show the highest attrition levels, indicating areas where HR intervention may be needed most.

-Business Impact

This dashboard helps organizations:
Reduce employee turnover through early identification of attrition patterns
Improve employee retention strategies
Enhance HR decision-making with real-time workforce insights
Identify departments and employee groups needing immediate attention
Support strategic planning and workforce optimization

Overall, the dashboard transforms raw HR data into meaningful business insights that help organizations build a more stable and productive workforce.
